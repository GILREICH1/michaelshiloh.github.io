Relational Aesthetics Workshop at Maker Faires

Over the course of the first four San Francisco Maker Faires (2006-2009) I ran a giant Relational Aesthetics event in which tons (literally) of discarded machinery, broken toys and games, scrap fabric and other materials (including radar absorbing foam used in stealth aircrafts) were consumed by hundreds of visitors and converted into new objects
