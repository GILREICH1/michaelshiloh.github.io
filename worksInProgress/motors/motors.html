Motors

Motors is a musical instrument consisting of many different motors and motor mechanisms installed around a space. The motors are not embellished with any additional sound making devices, so that the sound heard is only that of the motor and the mechanism. Motors and mechanisms are preferably salvaged or found, but new motors and mechanisms are used as well. Each device is individually controlled via MIDI.

Motors is not a stand-alone structure; rather, it is installed in a space: along the walls, hanging from the ceiling, and on the floor. Some structures may be used as well.

Like other musical instruments, the true value of this instrument is the music it produces. I collaborate with composers who would compose a piece specifically for the site.
